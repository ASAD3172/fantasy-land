About Our Park

Fantasy Land Amusement Park has been a beacon of joy and excitement for families and thrill-seekers for over 50 years. Our mission is to provide an unforgettable experience filled with adventure, laughter, and magical moments for guests of all ages.

We are dedicated to maintaining the highest standards of safety and cleanliness, ensuring a worry-free environment where you can focus on making memories. Our team of passionate staff works tirelessly to bring you innovative new attractions and classic favorites year after year.

Come and discover why Fantasy Land is more than just a park—it's a world of pure imagination!

About Water Management

Using state of the art filtration systems that ensure maximized water conservation.

All pool water resources in the water park are re-circulated, filtered, treated and re-used.

Pool Water systems are separated to avoid cross contamination.

Policies and procedures are in place to reduce the risk of contamination.
